About CHARGE

CHARGE is the prestigious flagship technical event organized by The Electronix Club of the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering, Sreenidhi Institute of Science and Technology (SNIST). It stands as a symbol of innovation, collaboration, and excellence in the field of electronics and technology, a grand celebration that unites passionate engineers, innovators, and thinkers under one electrifying platform.

With a remarkable legacy of over 10 years, CHARGE has continually inspired generations of students to explore beyond textbooks, transforming creative ideas into real-world solutions. Through workshops, project exhibitions, competitions, and interactive experiences, it has evolved into a hub of technical brilliance that reflects the true spirit of The Electronix Club — fostering innovation, teamwork, and the pursuit of engineering excellence.
