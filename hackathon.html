About The Hackathon

THE ELECTRONIX CLUB (TEC) invites you to participate in the CHARGE Hackathon — a specialized event dedicated to core electronics and communication engineering.

This event provides a platform for students to demonstrate their expertise in hardware-software co-design. Participants will be judged on circuit efficiency, power optimization, and system robustness.

Key Domains

  • Embedded Systems: Microcontroller-based solutions.
  • VLSI: Digital and Analog IC design concepts.
  • IoT: Building the bridge between physical sensors and the digital cloud.
  • Robotics: Mechanical design and sensor integration.
  • Communication: Data transmission and RF engineering.
