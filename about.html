About CHARGE

Organized by The Electronix Club, Department of ECE, SNIST, CHARGE is the flagship technical festival that unites innovation, technology, and creativity under one electrifying platform.

Photo Gallery

The Legacy of CHARGE

Over the years, CHARGE has evolved into one of SNIST’s most celebrated technical gatherings, inspiring thousands of students to explore the future of electronics, embedded systems, and innovation. Through its wide range of events — from workshops and paper presentations to project expos and hackathons — it fosters a culture of practical learning and technological excellence.

The event not only provides a stage for innovation but also builds a community of passionate engineers ready to lead the world of emerging technologies.
